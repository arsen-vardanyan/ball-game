HOW TO PLAY?

    You play with a blue rectangle. It is controled with «A» (left) and «D» (right), or ← and → keyboard buttons.

    When the ball hits the top of the board, your scores will increase by one. You need to earn as many points as possible. Each time the ball hits the blue rectangle, the ball speed will gradually increase. When the ball hits the bottom of the board, you lose.

    The purple rectangle is your opponent, which is computer controlled. It intercepts the ball road, preveting the ball from hitting the top of the board.

    By picking an orange box falling from the top, the size of your ball will either randomly increase, or randomly decrease, or remain the same, or it will come to its original size. It falling every 15 - 25 seconds.

    The black numbers on the bottom left indicate your current score.

    The red numbers on the bottom right indicate the highest score you have earned.

    And the brown numbers in the bottom center are your playing time.
